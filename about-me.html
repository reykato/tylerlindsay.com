Racing

Since the age of 5, I have been driving race cars alongside my family pit crew. Traveling across the Eastern half of the US, I have raced thousands of races and won hundreds, with prizes up to $1200. Through the years, I've moved up to larger and faster cars. Today, I primarily race in a class called the 600 Micro Sprints. The cars are custom-built and repurpose sport motorcycle engines. Alongside the 600 Micro Sprint, I also race an asphalt car in the TQ Midget class. I typically race on dirt oval tracks with the 600 Micro Sprints, but in the Winter some large TQ Midget events are held indoors on asphalt. To learn more about my racing career, visit my facebook page or check out some racing video on my YouTube channel!

Snowboarding

Snowboarding is another hobby/sport I've had since my childhood. The feeling of flying down the mountain and tearing through powdered snow is hard to replicate! In the winter of 2021, I went on a group trip to Killington, VT. Not only was the trip incredibly fun, but it sparked my interest in winter travel. My destination for 2022 was Mont-Tremblant, Quebec, Canada. Not only was it my first time outside the country, it was also my first time traveling with my partner Aidan! Despite my lack of coherent French speaking, we had a great time, also enjoying two days in Montreal on the way to and from. I occasionally post about snowboarding on my Instagram profile.
